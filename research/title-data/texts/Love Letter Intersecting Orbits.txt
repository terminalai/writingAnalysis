They met when she was 45 and he was 32. They fell quickly for one another, celebrating every equinox and solstice by making love outside.

She had three children from a former marriage. He wanted to get married and have kids of his own.

In this week’s Modern Love essay, Rosemary Howe Camozzi discusses age differences, life trajectories and the lasting meaning in transient relationships.
